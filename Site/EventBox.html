Greater Craigmillar Community Campfire Event

Join us for an unforgettable evening at the Greater Craigmillar Community Campfire Event on the 5th of November! Gates open at 5 PM, and the night will be filled with exciting entertainment for all ages. Enjoy live music, fire dancers, and the warmth of campfire-themed food, including hot stoves and hot chocolate. Get cozy around the fire pits, toast some marshmallows, and marvel at the stunning woodland light show and a spectacular projector display of fireworks.
